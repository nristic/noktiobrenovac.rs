Account takeover is when a fraudster gains unauthorized access to your users’ accounts. Cybercriminals use a variety of methods to steal a user’s credentials, including social engineering, guessing, spear phishing and keystroke logging.

The easiest and most effective way to protect against account takeover is to use two-factor authentication. With this enabled, a fraudster would not only need to have the user’s credentials, they would also need to get their hands on your user’s phone.

TeleSign Verify SMS

Authenticate users by sending a verification code via SMS.

TeleSign Verify Voice

Send a voice call to the registered phone number to confirm the user’s identity.

TeleSign AuthID Kit

Easily embed advanced authentication into any existing app to protect user accounts and verify transactions.
